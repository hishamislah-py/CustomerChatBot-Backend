# Viewing BIN and Card Ranges

The BIN & Card Ranges menu on the [Apata Portal Dashboard](Apata_dashboard.htm#Apata_Portal_Dashboard) enables you to view details of BIN and cards ranges that have been set up in the system.

![](../Resources/Images/Apata/BIN_card_ranges.png "Viewing card ranges")

Figure 39: View BIN & Card Ranges

To view details of a card range, click the ![](../Resources/Images/Apata/down_arrow.png "Expand") icon. See the example below.
