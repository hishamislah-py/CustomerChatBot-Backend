### Completing your Chip Parameters Validation

The Card Schemes require validation of your card chip personalisation. The process is called Visa PVT (Personalisation Validation Testing) or Mastercard CPV (Card Personalisation Validation). The CPV is used to verify that all chip devices meet the Scheme's personalisation standards. CPV includes various aspects such as EMV data preparation, key management systems, and secure PIN administration.

Thredd recommend you refer to this guide before completing your chip validation form, as it contains details specific to the Thredd platform.
